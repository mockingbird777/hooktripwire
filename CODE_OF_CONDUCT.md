# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We are committed to a welcoming, harassment-free community for everyone, regardless of identity, background, experience, or ability. Security discussions can involve disagreement; they must remain respectful, evidence-based, and focused on improving the project.

## Expected behavior

- Be considerate, patient, and constructive.
- Assume good intent while evaluating technical claims on their evidence.
- Respect privacy and coordinated disclosure.
- Accept feedback gracefully and acknowledge others’ contributions.
- Help make participation accessible to newcomers.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, discrimination, threats, personal attacks, sexualized conduct, deliberate intimidation, doxxing, publishing private vulnerability details without coordination, and sustained disruption are not tolerated.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately to the maintainers through the repository owner’s GitHub contact channel. Maintainers may edit or remove contributions and temporarily or permanently restrict participation when behavior threatens the safety or health of the community. Reports will be handled as confidentially as reasonably possible.

This policy applies in project spaces and when someone publicly represents the project.
